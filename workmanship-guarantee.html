Workmanship Guarantee

This installation has been completed by Paul the Tiler and is covered by a 12-month workmanship guarantee, provided the work has been carried out in line with correct preparation and installation methods.

What this covers
  • Tile installation
  • Adhesion related to workmanship
  • Grouting and silicone application
What this does not cover
  • Structural movement or building defects
  • Pre-existing substrate issues
  • Movement from timber floors or unsuitable surfaces where advised
  • Work carried out against professional recommendation
Important

This guarantee is based on the installation being completed using the correct preparation, materials, and methods in line with recognised tiling standards.

Please note: This is a workmanship guarantee only. It does not cover product failure, accidental damage, impact damage, misuse, or issues caused by movement or defects outside the tiled installation itself.
